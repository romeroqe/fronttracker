FrontTracker

FrontTracker is a Python library designed for the detection and characterization of oceanic fronts from gridded datasets. Ocean fronts are regions of strong horizontal gradients in temperature, salinity, or other tracers, and they play a key role in ocean dynamics, climate variability, and ecosystem processes
